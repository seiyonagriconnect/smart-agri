Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Once you subscribe to a plan, go to the order page and choose your veggies. Just make sure to place your order 48 hours in advance!

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes! You can cancel, upgrade, or pause your plan anytime from your account dashboard.

When will my order be ready for pickup?

Orders placed 48 hours in advance will be ready at your selected pickup point on the delivery day.

📜 Terms and Conditions

  • 📦 Bulk orders are subject to availability and stock limits at the pickup location.
  • ⏳ All orders must be placed at least 48 hours in advance for guaranteed processing.
  • 🚫 Limit of 1 order per 12 hours per subscription user unless otherwise specified in your plan.
  • 🧾 Prices are fixed for your selected plan duration and may not fluctuate.
  • 📍 Pickup only at listed partner stores. Delivery is currently not available.
